About Our Website


We created this website for all users that want to have a healthier life by having a better diet. Having a healthy diet is not easy, but this website allows users to input information for a personalized and accurate healthy yet tasty diet due to their preferences. We do this by collecting the information you input to find recipes or meals adequate to a users likings to further improve meals. A user will get three meals everyday, including breakfast, lunch, and dinner.


OUR GOALS

Accessibility

The best and most efficient way for someone to interact with our website is to create an account and log in. By creating this online space users are able to recieve meals in their feed for breakfast, lunch, and dinner in no time.

Health Insights

Another goal at Foodverse is to provide the most accurate information regarding any health concerns. At Foodverse we create the tools necessary to gather data and lead our clients into a healthy lifestyle.

User Satisfactory

At Foodverse, we always aim to satisfy our users. It is our responsibility to provide the best service by establishing good relationships with our clients.
